World-Class Training Under One Roof

Welcome to Amir Academy of Martial Arts — a premier, high-end MMA and weight training facility built from the ground up for serious athletes and everyday warriors alike. Located in the heart of St. Petersburg, Florida, our state-of-the-art gym is more than just a place to work out — it's a combat sports destination designed to push your limits and transform your potential.

Step inside and you'll find a full-size professional boxing ring at the center of it all, surrounded by over 15 heavy punching bags ready for striking drills, conditioning rounds, and technical work. Our extensive collection of sparring gear, focus mitts, Thai pads, and training equipment ensures that every fighter — from first-timers to seasoned competitors — has everything they need to sharpen their skills.

The facility features three distinct training rooms, each purpose-built for different disciplines. Whether you're rolling on our competition-grade mats for Jiu-Jitsu, perfecting your striking combinations in the boxing area, or grinding through a high-intensity MMA session, every space is optimized for performance, safety, and focus.

Beyond combat sports, our fully equipped weight training and cardio section boasts a comprehensive range of free weights, machines, resistance equipment, and cardio machines — everything you need to build strength, improve endurance, and complement your martial arts training with a complete fitness regimen.

Every detail of Amir Academy has been carefully curated to create an environment where champions are made. From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll feel the energy of a gym that has produced elite fighters and hosted legendary events like the Battle of Tampa Bay for over 27 years. This isn't your average gym — this is where dedication meets world-class resources, and where your journey to greatness begins.
